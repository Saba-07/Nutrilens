Malnutrition is a significant challenge affecting millions of children, impacting their growth, development, and overall well-being. The Mid-Day Meal Scheme, initiated by the government, aims to provide nutritious meals to schoolchildren, ensuring they receive the necessary daily nutrients. However, ensuring the quality and nutritional adequacy of these meals remains a challenge. Our AI-driven system is designed to analyze food images from mid-day meals, estimate caloric value and essential nutrients, and help authorities monitor whether children are receiving a balanced and adequate diet.
